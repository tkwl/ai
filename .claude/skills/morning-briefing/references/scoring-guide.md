# Scoring Guide

## Scale Interpretation

- **90-100**: Exceptional. Actively exceeded expectations. Rare.
- **70-89**: Strong. Clearly lived this dimension today.
- **50-69**: Adequate. Did the minimum or showed some effort.
- **30-49**: Weak. Missed opportunities, fell into old patterns.
- **10-29**: Poor. Actively worked against this dimension.
- **0-9**: Absent. Zero effort or engagement.

## Dimension-Specific Criteria

### Entrepreneur (not IC)
- 90+: Delegated, strategized, sold. Zero hands-on fulfillment before video.
- 70: Mix of strategy and execution, but led with entrepreneurial thinking.
- 50: Did IC work but had some strategic moments.
- 30: Spent the day doing fulfillment, no leverage.
- 10: Pure execution, no delegation or strategy whatsoever.

### Sharing Value Publicly
- 90+: Recorded video AND posted content AND engaged publicly.
- 70: Recorded video or posted meaningful content.
- 50: Shared something but low effort or private.
- 30: Thought about it but didn't do it.
- 10: Zero public output.

### Relationships & Networking
- 90+: Proactive outreach, meaningful conversations, new connections.
- 70: Responded to people, maintained relationships.
- 50: Some interaction but mostly reactive.
- 30: Isolated, didn't reach out.
- 10: Avoided people or turned down opportunities to connect.

### Kind/Patient/Not Anxious (Pius)
- 90+: Calm, generous, present in conversations. Zero reactive moments.
- 70: Mostly patient, occasional stress but managed well.
- 50: Some anxiety or impatience showed.
- 30: Reactive, short-tempered, or visibly anxious.
- 10: Scarcity mindset dominated the day.

### Fun & People Want to Be Around
- 90+: People sought him out. Brought energy to interactions.
- 70: Pleasant, engaged, enjoyable to be around.
- 50: Neutral presence.
- 30: Low energy, withdrawn, or tense.
- 10: People avoided or walked on eggshells.

### Stability (emotional, financial, actions)
- 90+: Consistent behavior all day. Predictable in a good way. Financially disciplined.
- 70: Mostly stable with minor wobbles.
- 50: Some inconsistency but recovered.
- 30: Erratic behavior or impulsive decisions.
- 10: Full sprint/crash pattern or emotional volatility.

### Father (present, safety net)
- 90+: Quality time with John & Jack. Engaged, present, fun.
- 70: Spent time together, was available.
- 50: Around but distracted or half-present.
- 30: Minimal interaction.
- 10: Absent or distracted the entire day.

### Health Protocol
- 90+: Hit all targets: training, nutrition, sleep, HRV protocol.
- 70: Most targets hit, one missed.
- 50: Trained but nutrition/sleep off, or vice versa.
- 30: Skipped training or way off nutrition.
- 10: No effort toward health goals.

## Overall Score

Weighted average of all 8 dimensions. No rounding up. If data is missing for a dimension, note it and score conservatively (don't assume the best).
